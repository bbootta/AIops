## Review Checklist

- [ ] Every material claim in the brief has a Claim ID.
- [ ] Basel and regulatory claims use official sources first.
- [ ] News claims separate publication date and event date.
- [ ] Conflicting evidence is captured.
- [ ] Low-confidence claims are labeled or removed.
- [ ] All URLs and document titles were verified during the current research run.
